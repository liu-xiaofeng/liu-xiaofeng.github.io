I am currently an Instructor [faculty] at the Harvard Medical School (HMS) / Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH) affiliate with the Gorden Center for Medical Imaging, working with Prof. Jonghye Woo and Georges El Fakhri.

My recent works include deep learning for computer vision, pattern recognition and their application on medical data. I am also interested in a variety of hard-core problems in deep learning.

I was a Research Fellow at BIDMC/HMS. Before that, I was a joint supervision PhD at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) co-advised by Prof. Jane You, Prof. B.V.K Vijaya Kumar and Prof. Ping Jia [2014-2019]. I got my B.Eng in Automation, B.A. in Communication, and Minor in Biology from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) [2010-2014].

FYI my Chinese name is 刘(Liu) 小沣(Xiaofeng) /liou hsiao feng/.
