My recent works include deep learning for computer vision, pattern recognition and their application on medical data. I am also interested in a variety of hard-core problems in deep learning.

Before I join Harvard, I was a joint supervision PhD at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) co-advised by Prof. Jane You, Prof. B.V.K Vijaya Kumar and Prof. Ping Jia [2014-2019]. I got my B.Eng in Automation, B.A. in Communication, and Minor in Biology from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) [2010-2014].

FYI my Chinese name is 刘(Liu) 小沣(Xiaofeng) /liou hsiao feng/.

Closely collaborating with C.-C. Jay Kuo@USC, Jerry L. Prince@JHU and Yoshua Bengio@MILA
