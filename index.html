Bio

Research Fellow at Harvard

Works in deep learning and computer vision.

2014 - 2019 @UCAS/CMU PhD in EE 2010 - 2014 @USTC B.Eng in Automation B.A. in Communication Minor in Biological

I am currently a Research Fellow at Harvard Medical School(HMS), and the lead scientist of AI and research at Fanhan Tech co.

I am a joint supervision PhD in ECE of the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and Carnegie Mellon University (2016-2019) co-advised by Prof. Jane You, Prof. B.V.K Vijaya Kumar and Prof. Ping Jia. Concurrently as a research intern at Google / Facebook research, and collaborative closelly with Harry Yang, Yang Zou, Gerry Che, Zhiding Yu, Prof.Zhenhua Guo, Prof. Jay Kuo, Prof. Yoshua Bengio et al.

Before that, I got my B.Eng in Automation, B.A. in Communication, and Minor in Biology in the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

My recent works include image recognition/generation, uncertainty measurement, disentanglement, domain adaptation and diagnose system. I am also interested in a variety of hard-core problems in deep learning.

FYI my Chinese name is 刘(Liu) 小沣(Xiaofeng) /liou hsiao feng/
