I am a Faculty (Instructor) at Harvard and MGH. My recent works include hard-core deep learning, computer vision, pattern recognition and their application on medical image analysis.

I work with Prof. Jonghye Woo and Prof. Georges El Fakhri in Gordon Center for Medical Imaging. Closely collaborating with C.-C. Jay Kuo@USC, Yoshua Bengio@MILA, and Jerry L. Prince@JHU.

I was a research fellow at BIDMC, HMS for neural networks and learning system. Before I join Harvard, I was a joint supervision PhD at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and Dept. of Electrical and Computer Eng. at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) co-advised by Prof. Jane You, Prof. B.V.K Vijaya Kumar and Prof. Ping Jia [2014-2019]. I got my B.Eng in Automation, B.A. in Communication, and Minor in Biology from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) [2010-2014].

FYI my Chinese name is 刘(Liu) 小沣(Xiaofeng) /liou hsiao feng/.
