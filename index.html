Bio

5th year PhD candidate at UCAS/CMU

Works in deep learning and computer vision.

2014 - 2019 @UCAS/CMU PhD in ECE 2010 - 2014 @USTC B.Eng in Automation B.A. in Communication Minor in Bio

I am currently research intern at BIDMC Harvard Medical School.

Before this I did my B.Eng in Automation, B.A. in Communication, and Minor in Biology in the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am a joint supervision PhD candidate in the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) co-advised by Prof. Jane You, Prof. B.V.K Vijaya Kumar and Prof. Ping Jia. Concurrently as a research assosiate in ECE, CMU and research intern at Google / Facebook research.

My recent works include face/facial expression recognition, image generation and optics design. I am also interested in a variety of hard-core problems in deep learning.

FYI my Chinese name is 刘(Liu) 小沣(Xiaofeng) /liou hsiao feng/
